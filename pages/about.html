About Srimatrey Foods

A journey that we embarked in the year 2012 has had a steadfast growth in the food industry. Srimatrey has embarked the taste of Indian households with the flavor of South. Passed through generations, the recipes used in bringing these delicacies to you are the testament of Grandma's recipes. A legacy that is build in honesty and by sustaining the balance of natural flavors. All our products are very well accepted by one and all and are also exported to countries like USA, London, Australia, Singapore and Dubai.

Materials are sourced organically and catered to your needs. We at Srimatrey hold quality and accountability as our core values of serving you. We are constantly innovating and bringing out variations with food while also holding the traditional taste and flavors.

We at Srimatrey, apart from food products also had a wide range of other products like – herbal Hair wash and bath Powder, 35 herbs Agarbattis, Sambrani Agarbattis, pure Cow and Buffalo ghee, pure forest honey, medicinal value based turmeric and scented and fragrance candles.
